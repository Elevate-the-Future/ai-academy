About Us

We truly believe that AI is the new electricity and will transform everything we do – just like Electricity did it 100 years ago. We wanted to make sure the upcoming generation is prepared for this shift. It is with this intent that we started AI Academy. There is a lot of material available online but we felt real learning would be more effective if done interactive and so we have structured our offerings to be in person (class/zoom) lessons. Our focus has been to not just teach students concepts of artificial intelligence/machine learning but also apply it to actual implementation and real-life problem-solving. Students do many exercises and a group project at the end of the course to apply all they have learned. We hope our work creates excitement and love for this technology for the students.
