About Us

We believe that just like Electricity did 100 years ago, Artificial Intelligence is going to be part of and change everything around us. We wanted to make sure the upcoming generation is prepared for this shift. It is with this intent that we started AI Academy.

There is a lot of material available online but we felt real learning would be more effective if done interactive and so we have structured our offerings to be in person (class/zoom) lessons.

Our focus has been to not just teach students concepts of artificial intelligence/machine learning but also how to apply it to real-life problem-solving. Students do many exercises and a group project at the end of the course to apply all they have learned.

We have been teaching upper-middle school and high school students since the fall of 2018. We have also taught international students at San Jose State University. We hope our work creates excitement and love for this technology for the students.
